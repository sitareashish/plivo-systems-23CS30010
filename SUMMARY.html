Approach

  • FECPer K=2 frames: 2 DATA packets + 1 XOR-parity; receiver rebuilds any single lost packet from the survivors. No retransmission.
  • Why not duplicationOverhead scored vs raw n×160 payload, so 2 payloads/packet is already 2.0× before headers. Parity amortises to 1.55×.
  • Jitter bufferSole-reader receive thread; sequence-indexed buffer + FEC recovery, absorbs reorder & duplicates.
  • Playout threadIn-order, waits on a condition_variable until present or 2 ms before deadline. Self-clocking.

Key trade-off

Interleaving (FEC_STRIDE>1) hardens bursts but adds up to S×20 ms recovery latency; since the score rewards low delay it hurt (mild burst @60 ms: stride 1 = 1.25% vs stride 4 = 3.00%). Shipped default: FEC_STRIDE=1.
